About Our Mission

Our Story

Full Pantry began when we noticed food waste in our community while many struggled with hunger. WeFull Pantry began when students noticed food being wasted while families struggled with hunger. We created a simple solution: collecting surplus food from stores and restaurants and distributing it to those in need. What started as a campus project has grown into a community movement, proving that small actions can create big change in the fight against hunger. created a bridge between surplus and need. it was so sad to see people and children suffering all the time and so we decided to do something about it

Our Values

  • Compassion: Everyone deserves access to nutritious food
  • Sustainability: Reducing food waste helps our planet
  • Community: We believe in neighbors helping neighbors
  • Dignity: We provide food with respect and care

Our Team

We are students and community volunteers passionate about making a difference. From food collection to distribution, every hand makes an impact.

Future Goals

We aim to expand to more neighborhoods and establish permanent food distribution centers across the city.

We redistribute surplus food to eliminate hunger in our community.
